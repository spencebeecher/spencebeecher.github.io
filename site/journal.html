February 16, 2026 (Sunday)

Spent the morning listening to the Justice audiobook on Spotify—learned about Aristotle's definition of justice, which centers on things fulfilling their "natural" purpose. This doesn't hold much water on inspection. The argument that the purpose of politics is to produce 'good people' is interesting though. At the start of the lecture series, I thought of myself as a utilitarian—that justice maximizes benefit. Now I'm not so sure. Rawls makes good arguments that justice should maximize fairness. I'll write more on this later.

Later, I taught my wife how to vibe code for the first time and caught up on some work.

Wrapped up the day with a relaxing pool session with the dogs.
